What We Do

Apollo Staffing is a full service employment agency that assists with finding the right people for the right job. That right job can be for a few days, several months, or temp to permanent. We also offer payroll processing and on-site staffing management.

With offices in Covington, Savannah, and Cartersville, Apollo Staffing is able to service Georgia and South Carolina. We have thousands of qualified applicants on file at any given time for all types of jobs from light industrial jobs to clerical. Call us today to learn more.
